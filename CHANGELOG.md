- Random whisper on Power Infusion cast
- Configurable message pool (one per line)
- Audience filtering: Everyone, Guild, Battle.net friends, Guild+BNet, Whitelist, Blacklist
- Allow/deny messaging yourself
- Test button (whispers yourself; Shift-click whispers target)
- `/powerwords` and `/pw` slash commands to open config
